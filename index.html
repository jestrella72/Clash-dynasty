Clash Dynasty — Rules

🎯 Win Condition

Reduce your opponent's lives to 0. Each player has 4 lives (Storage Zone holds max 4 cards). When Storage is empty — you lose.

🃏 Deck Building

  • Max 40 cards per deck.
  • Must be mono-element (e.g. Water only).
  • Dual-element characters (e.g. Water/Electricity) are allowed in their matching deck.

🗺️ Field Mat Zones

  • Character Field — 4 characters max per player
  • Slot — your element tokens (無極)
  • Storage Zone — 4 cards max = your lives
  • Field Card — bottom left of your mat
  • Main Deck — bottom right of your mat
  • Trash — destroyed/discarded cards (top right of Character Field)

🔄 Turn Phases

  • Beginning: Draw 5 cards (turn 1 only). Gain 1 Element. Option to sacrifice Storage → take 1 life from opponent.
  • Draw: Draw 1 card.
  • Action: Play characters, events, triggers. Attack.
  • Ending: End-of-Turn Field effects only.

⚔️ Combat

  • Characters must be Charged to attack. They become Discharged after.
  • Can attack a specific opponent character, or their Field Card.
  • Attacking a character: defender can block with Storage or let the character take the hit.
  • Attacking the Field Card: defender can block with Storage or lose 1 life.
  • Trashing a character does NOT cost the opponent a life.
  • At 0 HP → sent to Trash.
